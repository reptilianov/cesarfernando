I write, draw, and take notes. This site is my public notebook: a space where fiction, essay, sketch, and speculation converge.

I have no editorial plan. I follow no niche. This place exists because I need a site where all my voices can coexist without asking each other for permission.

**What you'll find here:**

- **Writing** — novel fragments, short stories, and essays in progress
- **Notebook** — notes on occultism, symbols, and readings
- **Drawings** — finished illustrations and sketches
- **Scratches** — political opinions and loose thoughts
- **Process** — documentation of my creative method

If something you read here resonates with you, write to me. Conversations that begin in the margins tend to be the best ones.
